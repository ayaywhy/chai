All about our Chai Retreats

In the busyness of life, it's easy to lose track of our spiritual growth. Here, you'll find a peaceful space to reflect, recharge, and deepen your connection with Allah.

With a carefully planned schedule, you'll experience personal growth, sisterhood, and mindfulness. By fully engaging in the retreat, you’ll leave with lasting memories, deeper connections, and a refreshed spirit, in sha Allah.

Come with an open heart and the intention to grow closer to Allah. May this journey be a source of blessing and worship. Ameen.

About Our Instructor and Guide

Malama Hafsatu Isa Waziri is a distinguished leader, educator, and community builder with over 20 years of experience in public service and Islamic education. She serves as the Assistant Commander General of the Kano State Hisbah Board, promoting Islamic values and community well-being.

Her background includes a B.A. in Arabic/Islamic Studies Phonology and a Postgraduate Diploma in Education from Bayero University, Kano. Hafsatu has received accolades for her work, including the Family Mentor Award and a Certificate of Excellence for her contributions to education and family counseling.

Throughout her career, she has held leadership roles in various educational institutions and has been a guide for pilgrims on both state and federal levels. Her dedication to uplifting the less privileged through education, counseling, and mentorship shines through in all her endeavors.

Malama Hafsatu is passionate about fostering personal growth, and her guidance during this retreat will provide invaluable spiritual and educational insights.

Retreat Itinerary

Embark on a 12-day spiritual journey to rediscover yourself, strengthen your connection with Allah, and bond with like-minded sisters. Guided by the wisdom of Malama Hafsatu Isa Waziri, this transformational experience offers personalized support and insights every step of the way. The retreat spans three holy cities, each offering unique spiritual opportunities.

Jeddah | 2 Days | 1st - 2nd February

Accommodations: Swissotel Living Jeddah (With breakfast)

  • Day 1: Unlock Your Dua Workshop, Evening dolphinarium visit, Seaside dinner experience
  • Day 2: Know Your Umrah Workshop, Transfer to Makkah

Makkah | 4 Days | 3rd - 6th February

Accommodations: Hilton Makkah (With breakfast)

  • Day 1: Perform Umrah
  • Day 2: Morning Halaqa
  • Day 3: Morning Halaqa, Museum visit, Hike up the Cave of Hira (with snacks) for those able
  • Day 4: Morning Halaqa

Madinah | 4 Days | 7th - 10th February

Accommodations: Pullman Zamzam Madinah (With breakfast)

  • Day 1: Ziyara & Museum visit
  • Day 2: Morning Halaqa, Women’s health interactive session with a specialist
  • Day 3: Morning Halaqa, Time management and Tarbiyah
  • Day 4: Morning Halaqa, Gratitude circle
